ArUco & AprilTag

Scanner and pattern generator — ArUco 4×4 and AprilTag 36h11, runs in the browser.

Scanner (camera) Pattern generator

Use PC + phone for a bit of fun: show a pattern on one device, open the scanner on the other and point the camera at it — instant mini AR game.

Powered by (used in this app)

  • js-aruco2 (Damiano Falcioni) — ArUco detection in the browser (delivered via jsDelivr).
  • apriltag-js-standalone (ARENA) — AprilTag 36h11 detection (WASM, loaded from arenaxr.github.io).
