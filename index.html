Fiducial Marker Tools

Scanner and generator for AprilTag (36h11) and ArUco (4x4) — runs directly in the browser.

Create a marker on one screen, scan it with another device — instantly.

No apps, no backend, no tracking, no uploads — everything runs locally in your browser.
